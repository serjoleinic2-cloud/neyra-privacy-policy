Privacy Policy

Effective date:20.04.2026

1. Overview

Neyra is designed with privacy as a core principle.

The app works entirely offline and does not use any servers to collect, store, or process your personal data.

2. Data You Provide

All data you enter into Neyra remains on your device, including:

  • mood entries and emotional data
  • notes (text, voice, images)
  • practice usage data
  • insights and analytics generated by the app

This data:

  • is not transmitted anywhere
  • is not accessible to the developer
  • is not used for tracking or profiling

3. Local Processing

All features, including analysis, pattern detection, and recommendations, are performed locally on your device.

Neyra does not use cloud processing, external APIs, or third-party AI services.

4. No Accounts or Tracking

  • No account is required
  • No tracking is performed
  • No analytics systems are used

5. Third-Party Services

Neyra itself does not share your data. However, certain technical data may be processed by platform providers required to deliver the app.

This includes:

  • app distribution
  • subscription payments
  • device-level technical information

These processes are handled independently by platforms such as Google Play.

6. Payments

All purchases are processed via Google Play Billing.

Neyra does not have access to your payment information.

7. Data Storage

  • All data is stored locally on your device
  • You can delete the app at any time to remove your data
  • You may use local backup and restore features

8. Your Rights (EU Users)

If you are located in the European Union, you have rights under GDPR, including access and deletion.

Since Neyra does not store data externally, you can fully control your data directly on your device.

9. Age Requirement

Neyra is intended for users aged 18 and older.

10. Security

Your data security depends on your device. We recommend using device-level protection such as passcodes and encryption.

11. Changes

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time.

The latest version is always available at:
https://www.neyra-app.com
